T.Slade Assignment 01: Personal Data

This page presents a visualization of the data generated by my Uber usage. The majority of the data was generated in Nairobi, Kenya while commuting to and from my workplace; the remainder was generated during a trip to the U.S. for a conference and a school event.

The x-axis encodes the day of the week (1-7, where 1 = Sunday). The y-axis encodes the hour of the day (0-23, where 0 = midnight). The radius of each dot encodes the number of rides I took within that hour of that day (5px per ride).
